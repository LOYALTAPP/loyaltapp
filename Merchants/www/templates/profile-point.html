Enter the Points that will be credited / added to the users card.
e.g. If you enter Rupees = 100 and Points = 1. When a customer does a purchase or 100 rupees, 1 point will be credited in his/her card.
Enter the Points that will be debited / deducted from the users card.
e.g. If you enter Points = 1 and Rupees = 1. When a customer redeems his/her points, 1 point will be considered as 1 rupee.
